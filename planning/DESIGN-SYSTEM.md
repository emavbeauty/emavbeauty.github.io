# DESIGN SYSTEM

## Brand Feeling

The site should feel like a private botanical nail studio: soft, detailed, ethereal, grounded, and professional. The visual world should suggest a fairy garden without becoming childish or costume-like.

Keywords:

- Ethereal
- Whimsical
- Botanical
- Cottagecore
- Fairy-garden inspired
- Calm
- Artistic
- Trustworthy
- Luxurious but approachable

## Color Palette

Recommended palette:

- Warm Ivory: `#F8F3EA`
- Soft Lavender: `#DCCBEF`
- Mist Lavender: `#F0EAF7`
- Deep Plum: `#4A204F`
- Muted Purple: `#7B4A8E`
- Sage: `#A8B99A`
- Moss: `#5F714D`
- Pearl: `#F6F3F0`
- Ink: `#241B24`

Usage:

- Warm ivory for page backgrounds.
- Mist lavender for quiet section bands.
- Sage/moss for natural nail care and botanical accents.
- Deep plum for headings, buttons, and navigation.
- Pearl/silver details for subtle highlights.

Avoid:

- Heavy black backgrounds.
- Neon purple.
- Low-contrast pastel text.
- Purple-only layouts.

## Typography

Recommended approach:

- Display/heading font: elegant serif with good mobile readability.
- Body font: clean sans serif.
- Accent font: optional decorative script or handwritten style only for tiny labels, never paragraphs.

Rules:

- Body copy must remain highly readable on mobile.
- Avoid long italic passages.
- Avoid script navigation.
- Use generous line height.

## Layout

Principles:

- Mobile-first.
- Short paragraphs.
- Clear section headings.
- Organic but controlled whitespace.
- Strong image/copy rhythm.
- No cluttered decorative backgrounds behind important text.

Cards:

- Use cards for portfolio items, service summaries, testimonials, and FAQ groups.
- Keep border radii modest and polished.
- Avoid nested card layouts.

## Buttons

Primary button:

- Deep plum background
- Ivory text
- Botanical or pearl highlight on hover
- Strong focus ring

Secondary button:

- Transparent or ivory background
- Deep plum border/text
- Calm hover fill

Core labels:

- Join the Waitlist
- Explore Services
- View My Work
- Book an Appointment

## Decorative System

Use:

- Thin botanical line art
- Tiny star or pearl accents
- Soft vine dividers
- Subtle mist/light textures
- Organic image masks or frames only if performance remains strong

Do not use:

- Excessive sparkles
- Glitter filters
- Clip-art flowers
- Heavy gradients
- Stock nail photos

## Accessibility

Design must include:

- Minimum readable body size
- Visible focus states
- Sufficient contrast
- Alt text for meaningful images
- Empty alt for purely decorative graphics
- Reduced-motion-safe interactions
- No text-only color cues
