# Ema V Beauty Website Planning Summary

## Recommended Website Pages

The planned static website includes Home, Services, New Clients, Nail Art, Portfolio, About Ema, Policies & FAQ, Join Waitlist / Book, Contact, Privacy Policy, and a custom 404 page.

## Homepage Layout

The homepage should open with a clear natural-nail positioning hero for Ema V Beauty in Parker, Colorado, followed by specialty cards, client-fit guidance, new-client steps, nail art preview, portfolio preview, About Ema preview, studio location, policy confidence, and a final waitlist call to action.

## Brand Colors

The recommended palette is warm ivory, soft lavender, mist lavender, deep plum, muted purple, sage, moss green, pearl, and dark ink. Purple remains part of the brand, but the final site should balance it with botanical greens, warm neutrals, and softer ethereal tones.

## Typography Direction

Use an elegant readable serif for headings, a clean sans serif for body copy and navigation, and only very limited decorative accent typography. All typography should remain readable on mobile.

## Selected Signature Motion Effects

Recommended motion effects are a living botanical hero, a restrained growing vine or illustrated journey detail, refined scroll reveals, and interactive portfolio cards. A subtle booking-status highlight may also be used if it remains calm and accessible.

## Mobile And Reduced-Motion Behavior

The site should be mobile-first, keyboard accessible, and respectful of `prefers-reduced-motion`. Motion should simplify or become static on mobile and for reduced-motion users. Important content must remain visible if JavaScript fails.

## Information Still Needed

The build still needs confirmed booking and waitlist links, current booking status, exact services, prices, durations, nail art levels, policies, FAQ answers, testimonials, privacy details, and final approval of which supplied images should be used.
